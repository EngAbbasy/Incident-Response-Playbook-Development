# Literature Review

## Existing Frameworks

### 1. NIST SP 800-61
- 4 phases: Preparation, Detection, Analysis, Containment, Eradication, Recovery
- Widely adopted standard

### 2. SANS Incident Handler's Handbook
- 6 phases similar to our approach
- Practical checklists included

### 3. MITRE ATT&CK T1566 - Phishing
- Technique: Phishing
- Sub-techniques: Spearphishing, Spearphishing via Service
- Our playbook aligns with this framework
